A little about me

My name is Zahraa Akhtar, Currently in second year studying BSc(H) Web Technologies at The University of Huddersfield. My interest in computers has grown ever since my GCSE's which allowed consistent high grades and enabled me to develop my understanding further to Degree level.

I enjoy looking at all of the different parts of computing from data analysis to graphic design, they all really appeal to me. Having the ability to start creating a user interface from paper to Illustrator and then going on to coding based on that particular design. It really lets me run away with my artistic skills and sharpen my coding abilities. I relish designing just as much as I do coding.

This personal portfolio was created to really display my varying pieces of work from college, to my working career. This platform really helps to depict all the different strands of my work. My blog will be more personal, it will describe my thoughts and feelings on my life, technology etc.

At the University of Huddersfield I am the Publicity officer for the ISoc society. This allows me to look at different mediums of social platforms and make them better with both content and layout. It has taught me a lot about balancing my time, as it is essentially a full time job. The amazing thing about being a publicity officer aside from the design aspect, is the amount of socalising with lots of different people across the University that I wouldn't necessarily meet otherwise. Also the perks of the job mean I get to go to a verity of socials such as Ice Skating and Manchester for dinner with ISoc's across the country!

As a Technical Specialist on the Apple Genius Bar, I interact with many different people on a daily basis to help troubleshoot problems they may be having with their iOS devices. Working for Apple has really made me value the importance of team work, as without my amazing colleague's support I would not be able to be the technician I am today. Support works both ways and it's something I now really value when working anywhere it's an important part of any job. The help I give/get from my colleagues and customers alike is truly an amazing experience that allows me to work at my best and push myself to learn and develop more to support both myself and others in the team.

Now you have got to know me a little better why not browse through my portfolio and my work. Feel free to get in touch with any queries you have.
